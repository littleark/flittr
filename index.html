About flittr

Flittr tries to connect a random twitter with a picture on flickr with the same hidden meaning.
Flittr is addressed to all the people who don't like to spend time playing the solitaire game.
It's funny to see how a bunch of thoughts and a picture taken by two distant person can be connected.
Sit there and enjoy the gallery!
